What is EkoBar GFRP?

GFRP (Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer) is a high-performance composite material that is specifically designed for use in construction and infrastructure projects. GFRP originated in the aerospace and space-tech sectors, and is our eco-alternative to traditional steel reinforcement. It is a smart, 4x lighter, 2x stronger, 25% cheaper rust-proof alternative built for next-gen construction.
